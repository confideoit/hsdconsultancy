Contact FAQs

What services does ConfideoIT provide? ▶
ConfideoIT offers cloud computing training programs (AWS, DevOps, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes), professional web development services, cloud infrastructure solutions, DevOps consulting, and digital transformation services for businesses.
What are ConfideoIT's office hours? ▶
Our office is open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays, but our online support is available 24/7 for urgent queries.
How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry? ▶
We typically respond to email inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. Phone calls and WhatsApp messages are answered immediately during office hours. For urgent matters, please call directly.
